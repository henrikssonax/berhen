Our engagements 

Berhen Group's role is to be an active owner and we focus on activities that promote the best interests of our holdings in a long-term perspective. As a lead owner, Berhen Group works to ensure that the boards of holdings have the right composition and that our representatives on boards drive strategic work.

Berhen Group’s majority ownership shall be exercised in a committed and judicious manner. Berhen Group's team evaluate new opportunities that are in line with Berhen Group’s investment and ownership philosophy.
